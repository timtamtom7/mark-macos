Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Mark ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Mark handles your information when you use our macOS application.

Information We Collect

Mark is designed to be privacy-first. The app operates entirely on your local device.

Screen Recording Access

Mark requires macOS Screen Recording permission to draw annotation overlays on top of other applications. This permission is used only on your local machine. We do not capture, transmit, or store any screen content.

Annotations and Documents

Any annotations you create within Mark are stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud sync, your annotation presets are stored in your personal iCloud Drive container.

Cloud Upload (Imgur)

When you use the "Upload to Cloud" feature, annotated images are uploaded to Imgur's servers using their anonymous upload API. Images uploaded this way are subject to Imgur's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We do not control or store copies of uploaded images.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your annotation presets (tool preferences, colors, shortcuts) are stored in your iCloud account. This data is associated with your Apple ID and is subject to Apple's iCloud terms.

Usage Data

Mark does not collect any analytics, telemetry, or usage data from your device. We do not use any third-party analytics services.

How We Use Information

All information processed by Mark stays on your device or in your iCloud account. We do not access, review, or use your personal information for any purpose.

Data Retention

Annotation data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. iCloud data is retained according to your iCloud settings.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to delete. To remove iCloud-synced data, delete the Mark app from your iCloud-connected devices.

Children's Privacy

Mark is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
